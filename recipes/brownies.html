Brownies

Description

These Vegan Gluten-Free Brownies are the perfect indulgence for anyone with dietary restrictions, offering a rich and fudgy texture without sacrificing flavor. The recipe uses a combination of gluten-free flour, cocoa powder, and flaxseeds to create the structure of the brownies, while almond milk and coconut oil provide moisture and richness. With simple ingredients like maple syrup for sweetness and vanilla extract for depth, these brownies have a smooth, melt-in-your-mouth quality that’s sure to satisfy your chocolate cravings. The flaxseed works as a binder, mimicking the role of eggs in traditional recipes, and gives these brownies a unique, slightly nutty flavor.

The process is easy and straightforward, making these brownies a go-to for a quick, delicious treat. After combining the dry ingredients, you simply mix in the wet ingredients until everything is well incorporated. The batter is poured into a greased pan and baked until the edges are slightly firm, but the center remains soft and fudgy. Once cooled, the brownies can be cut into squares and enjoyed as is, or dressed up with a dusting of powdered sugar or a scoop of vegan ice cream. Whether you're following a vegan or gluten-free lifestyle or simply craving a decadent chocolate treat, these brownies are sure to be a hit.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons chia seed meal
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 cup raw sugar
  • ½ cup agave nectar
  • 3 tablespoons vegan margarine
  • 1 teaspoon gluten-free vanilla extract
  • ¾ cup brown rice flour
  • ½ cup almond meal
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly oil a baking dish.
  2. Beat chia meal and both amounts of water together in a bowl; let stand until thick, about 5 minutes. Blend raw sugar and agave into chia seed mixture with high speed blender. Add vegan margarine and vanilla extract; blend.
  3. Mix brown rice flour, almond meal, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl. Stir cocoa powder into the flour mixture. Add chia meal mixture and stir until combined; pour into prepared baking dish.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the top is dry and the edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan, 45 to 55 minutes.
