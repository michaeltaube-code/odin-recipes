Orange Muffins

Description

These Vegan and Gluten-Free Orange Muffins are a delightful treat that combines the natural sweetness of coconut sugar with the bright, citrusy flavor of fresh orange juice and zest. The recipe is straightforward, requiring only a blender to combine gluten-free rolled oats, almond milk, and a few pantry staples into a smooth batter. This simplicity makes it accessible for both novice and seasoned bakers.

Baked at 190°C (375°F) for about 22 minutes, the muffins emerge with a golden top that springs back when lightly pressed, indicating their perfect texture. Each muffin is under 200 calories, making them a wholesome option for breakfast or a snack. For those with dietary preferences, the recipe offers flexibility; any non-dairy milk can be substituted for almond milk, and the muffins can be enjoyed warm with a spread of vegan butter for added richness.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups gluten-free rolled oats
  • 1 cup coconut sugar
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons orange zest
  • 1 ½ teaspoons gluten-free baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C
  2. Combine all the ingredients
  3. Bake in the oven for 22 min
  4. Cool in the tin for 10 min
